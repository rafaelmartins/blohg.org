blohg-0.10
==========

.. tags: announcements, releases

blohg 0.10 was just released.

New features:

- Facebook_/`Google+`_ integration, using the `Open Graph`_ protocol.
- New ``include`` directive, to include files from the Mercurial repository.

.. _Facebook: http://www.facebook.com/
.. _`Google+`: http://plus.google.com/
.. _`Open Graph`: http://ogp.me/

This release also includes several bug fixes and documentation_ improvements.

.. _documentation: http://docs.blohg.org/

You may want to check the `upgrade notes`_.

.. _`upgrade notes`: http://docs.blohg.org/en/0.10/upgrade/

Enjoy!
